What is Coastal Spirit all about?

Founded in 1993 after an inspiring walk along the Cornish coast, we started with a simple mission: to create well-priced, well-made, and distinctive clothes that quickly become feel-good favourites.

Over 30 years later, we're still doing exactly that. A sense of fun, an eye for practicality, and a deep commitment to slow fashion—designing pieces you'll love for years, not just one season.

— The Coastal Spirit team
